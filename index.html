About

Xavier is a multilingual professional fluent in English, French, and Mandarin, with over 10 years of experience in product management and 2.5 years in management consulting. He has successfully built and exited two startups, demonstrating a strong ability to identify and monetize business opportunities in fast-paced environments.

As a global top 5% MBA candidate, Xavier completed his Rotman MBA with a focus on people leadership and product management. Following his MBA, he has held key roles at leading financial institutions, including Scotiabank and CIBC, where he has delivered strategic projects with high complexity.

Xavier is recognized for his ability to lead tough negotiations, make difficult yet strategic decisions, build consensus across stakeholders, and deliver innovative solutions that drive organizational growth.
