About Me

Product leader.
Strategic thinker.

Toronto, Canada

Xavier is a multilingual professional fluent in English, French, and Mandarin, with over 10 years of experience in product management and 2.5 years in management consulting. He holds PMP and MBA, as well as an associate degree in Computer Science.

He has successfully built and exited two startups, demonstrating a strong ability to identify and monetize business opportunities in fast-paced environments. As a global top 5% MBA candidate, Xavier completed his Rotman MBA with a focus on people leadership and product management.

Following his MBA, he has held key roles at leading financial institutions, including Scotiabank and CIBC, where he served as a product manager or management consultant and delivered strategic projects with high complexity.
