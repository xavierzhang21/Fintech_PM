Toronto · Canada

Xavier is a multilingual professional fluent in English, French, and Mandarin, with over 10 years of experience in product management and 2.5 years in management consulting. He holds a PMP certification, an MBA from Rotman (GMAT 740, global top 5%), and an Associate degree in Computer Science from Wilfrid Laurier University — a rare combination that bridges business strategy with hands-on technical execution.

He has successfully built and exited two startups: Les Escargots Education (30,000+ users, 40% annual revenue growth) and I Do Translation (sold for ~$400K USD). Following his MBA, Xavier has held key roles at Scotiabank and CIBC, delivering high-complexity strategic initiatives in retail banking and wealth management.

As Sr. Product Owner at CIBC Investor's Edge, he owns end-to-end onboarding for Canada's flagship self-directed trading platform — driving a 10% uplift in conversion, securing a Visa partnership projected to serve 150K+ customers annually with 200% ROI, and transforming the team's delivery model from Waterfall to Agile in under two months.
