Strategy & Transformation

Driving organizational transformation at CIBC — accelerating delivery, aligning stakeholders, and building a strategic partnership framework with measurable ROI.

Organization
CIBC Investor's Edge
Role
Sr. Product Owner, Onboarding
Period
2024 — 2025
Impact
30% faster delivery · 200% projected ROI

Context

As Sr. Product Owner at CIBC Investor's Edge, the mandate extended well beyond feature delivery. The team operated on a Waterfall model creating bottlenecks and slowing feedback loops. Simultaneously, an opportunity arose to explore a strategic partnership that could dramatically expand the platform's customer acquisition reach.

Waterfall→Agile in 2 months · 30% improvement in speed-to-market · Visa partnership initiated, projected to support 150K+ new customers/yr at 200% ROI in Year 1 · Monthly leadership showcase launched for senior visibility

Agile Transformation

  • Diagnosed root causes of delivery slowdown: unclear ownership, long approval chains, and quarterly planning cycles.
  • Designed and rolled out an Agile operating model — sprint ceremonies, product dashboard, and definition-of-done framework.
  • Achieved 30% improvement in speed-to-market within 2 months.
  • Launched a monthly leadership showcase to build stakeholder alignment proactively.

Vendor Strategy & Visa Partnership

  • Designed a vendor evaluation framework to identify partners that could enhance onboarding and expand customer reach.
  • Initiated and scoped a Visa partnership enabling new onboarding capabilities not previously possible.
  • Built the business case projecting 150K+ new customers/year and 200% ROI in Year 1 — approved and advanced to implementation.

Stakeholder Management

Navigated Legal, AML, Compliance, Operations, and Technology stakeholders simultaneously — each with different incentives and risk tolerances — to maintain alignment and ship on time.

Transformation playbook, vendor evaluation framework, and business case available upon request under NDA.
