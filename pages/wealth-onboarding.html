Wealth Onboarding — KYC & Compliance Automation

Designing an automated KYC pipeline that cut onboarding time by 60% while maintaining full regulatory compliance.

Organization FinTech
Role Product Manager
Domain Wealth Management / Compliance Technology
Type Product · KYC · Compliance · Automation

Overview

Know-Your-Customer (KYC) compliance is a fundamental requirement for financial institutions but historically one of the most friction-heavy parts of the customer journey. This project focused on designing an intelligent, automated KYC pipeline that dramatically reduced onboarding time while maintaining full regulatory compliance with FINTRAC and IIROC requirements.

Problem Statement

The existing KYC process relied heavily on manual document review and back-and-forth communication with customers to resolve verification issues. Average onboarding time ran to several business days, with a non-trivial percentage of applications requiring manual intervention.

Customers who encountered delays were significantly more likely to abandon the application or lose confidence in the platform — a direct revenue impact.

Solution Design

Working closely with compliance, legal, and engineering, I scoped a three-layer automation approach: (1) intelligent document capture with real-time quality checks, (2) automated identity verification via a third-party identity provider integrated into the product flow, and (3) a risk-scoring model to triage applications that genuinely required human review from those that could be auto-approved.

Critically, I designed the human review queue and exception-handling UX with the same care as the automated path — because edge cases are where trust is built or lost.

Results

  • 60% reduction in average time-to-verified-account
  • Significant decrease in manual review volume, freeing the compliance team for higher-complexity cases
  • Improved customer satisfaction scores on the onboarding journey
