Process Optimization & AI Tooling — CIBC Private Wealth

$300K in avoided professional fees, 60% productivity lift from AI tooling, and 47 pain points uncovered across 11 Ontario branches.

OrganizationCIBC · Commercial Banking & Wealth
RoleSr. Consultant, Process Optimization
PeriodJune 2024 – August 2025
Impact$300K Fees Avoided · 60% Productivity Lift

Overview

In this senior consulting role within CIBC's Commercial Banking and Wealth Management division, I led a broad process optimization mandate spanning Private Wealth applications including Wealthware, Salesforce, the Onboarding App (MyClient), and Wood Gundy Online. The role combined management consulting rigor with hands-on AI product delivery.

Management Consulting (60%)

I introduced a cost-benefit analysis model for validating all new initiatives across Private Wealth applications, and supported a bi-weekly Executives Steering Committee for decision-making. This framework alone helped achieve $300,000 in avoided professional fees within one year.

My most significant research effort involved a comprehensive field study across Ontario:

  • Visited 11 branches across the GTA, Barrie, Kingston, Oakville, Hamilton, London, Thornhill, and Mississauga
  • Conducted 31 qualitative interviews and collected 36 survey responses over 60 days
  • Synthesized findings into 47 pain points and 12 actionable initiatives to guide executive prioritization
  • Delivered structured process maps identifying major UX gaps across client onboarding (Process × People × Technology framework)

Field research revealed that technology training, troubleshooting support, and system stability were the top contributors to advisor dissatisfaction — scoring 5.3/10. Every percentage point of NPS improvement correlates to measurable revenue growth.

AI-Enabled Process Optimization (20%)

I built the Product Requirements Document and Business Case for an AI-powered disclaimer search tool, which drove a 60% productivity increase for Wood Gundy advisors — replacing a manual, multi-click disclaimer lookup process with an intelligent search that delivers all required disclaimers in one query.

I also drafted the migration strategy for over 21,000 documents from Wood Gundy to the new AI-powered Knowledge Management Platform (Knowledge Central). Additionally, I led GenAI use case development — coding GPT prompts to automate and streamline key business processes across the division.

Business Operations (20%)

I investigated frontline escalations for root-cause analysis, then designed a standardized triage funnel to separate high-impact issues from false alerts — ensuring the team's attention went to problems that genuinely mattered.

Key Deliverables

  • Cost-benefit analysis model adopted across all Private Wealth application initiatives
  • 47-pain-point research synthesis presented to the Executive Steering Committee
  • AI Disclaimer Search Tool PRD and business case (60% productivity lift)
  • 21,000-document KC migration strategy
  • Power BI Idea Hive dashboard tracking 118+ initiatives with categorization by LOB, application, and engagement

Process maps, cost-benefit models, and Power BI dashboard samples available upon request.
