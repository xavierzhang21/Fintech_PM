AI Process Optimization

Using AI tooling, deep branch research, and a Power BI idea management system to eliminate waste and drive organizational efficiency at CIBC Commercial Banking & Wealth.

Organization
CIBC Wood Gundy · Commercial Banking & Wealth
Role
Sr. Consultant, Process Optimization
Period
Jun 2024 — Aug 2025
Impact
$300K fees avoided · 60% productivity lift

The Problem

CIBC's Commercial Banking & Wealth division faced mounting inefficiencies: fragmented processes across branches, untracked improvement ideas, and heavy reliance on external consultants for work that internal teams could own.

$300K in professional fees avoided in 1 year · 11 branch visits · 24 interviews · 36 surveys · 45 pain points → 12 actionable initiatives

Discovery at Scale

  • Conducted 11 branch visits, 24 one-on-one interviews, and 36 structured surveys across the GTA.
  • Synthesized findings into 45 discrete pain points, triaged into 12 actionable improvement initiatives.
  • Built a comprehensive current-state process map as the baseline for change measurement.

AI-Powered Tooling

  • Built the PRD and business case for an AI-powered disclaimer search tool — delivering a 60% productivity lift for the advisor team.
  • Developed migration strategy for 21,000+ Knowledge Centre documents to the new AI-powered platform.
  • Led development of GenAI use cases, coding GPT prompts to automate and streamline business processes.

Power BI Idea Hive

Designed and built the "Idea Hive" — a Power BI dashboard capturing, tracking, and scoring improvement ideas across the division. 118+ ideas tracked with a custom ROI scoring model and live visibility for senior leadership.

Process maps, interview frameworks, and Power BI dashboard architecture available upon request under NDA.
