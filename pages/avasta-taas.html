Management Consulting: Training-as-a-Service

Designing a productized consulting offering from scratch — from market research through go-to-market — and launching a TaaS product that generated $112K gross revenue in its first month.

Organization
Avasta Incorporated
Role
Manager, Product Design & Management
Period
Jan 2021 — Mar 2022
Impact
$112K gross revenue in Month 1

The Opportunity

Avasta Incorporated wanted to move beyond project-based revenue toward a recurring, scalable product. The opportunity: productize Avasta's consulting expertise as a subscription training and enablement offering for mid-market clients. My mandate was end-to-end — define the product, design the go-to-market, and launch it.

$112K gross revenue generated in Month 1 — validating product-market fit and the TaaS model hypothesis.

Product Design

  • Led discovery interviews with target clients to identify skill gaps and training needs across their organizations.
  • Designed a modular curriculum architecture customizable per client vertical without rebuilding from scratch.
  • Developed pricing model, packaging tiers, and delivery format (live virtual, async, hybrid) to maximize revenue per engagement.

Go-to-Market & Launch

  • Built the sales collateral, onboarding playbook, and client success framework.
  • Coordinated across internal SMEs to develop course content, ensuring quality and consistency.
  • Led the launch sprint — securing initial clients, running first cohorts, and iterating on delivery based on feedback.

Result

The TaaS product generated $112K in gross revenue in its first month — exceeding the initial target and validating market appetite for a productized consulting-to-training model. This engagement demonstrated both product and commercial skills in a resource-constrained consulting environment.

Product design documentation, pricing model, and curriculum architecture available upon request under NDA.
