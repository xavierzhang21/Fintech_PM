Digital Global Business Payments

Owning the full digital product roadmap for ScotiaConnect — Scotiabank's business banking platform — delivering measurable revenue, conversion, and efficiency outcomes for 60,000 business customers.

Organization
Scotiabank
Role
Product Manager, Digital Channels
Period
Apr 2022 — May 2024
Impact
~$800K CAD/yr · 100% GIC conversion increase

Context

ScotiaConnect is Scotiabank's flagship digital banking platform for business customers — handling EFT payments, wire transfers, GIC investments, and cash management for 60,000+ Canadian businesses. As Product Manager for Digital Channels, I owned multiple feature streams simultaneously across a two-year engagement.

~500 manual hours/month eliminated · ~$800K CAD/yr revenue from Online Loan Statements · 100% GIC conversion increase · Wire success rate: 5%→80% · 6% EFT/Wire revenue increase · $12M annual funding secured via CIAM strategy

Online Loan Statements

  • Launched self-serve loan statements, eliminating ~500 manual hours/month of ops work.
  • Unlocked ~$800K CAD/year in revenue and reduced inbound support calls by ~400/month.

GIC Purchase Flow

  • Redesigned the GIC investment purchase flow end-to-end, achieving a 100% increase in conversion rate.
  • Applied journey mapping and drop-off analysis via Adobe Analytics to identify and eliminate friction.

Wire Payment Success Rate

  • Used Adobe Analytics to diagnose a critically low wire success rate (5%) caused by form validation errors.
  • Shipped targeted UX improvements raising the success rate to 80% — a 16× improvement and 6% revenue increase.

CIAM Strategy

Developed and presented the Customer Identity and Access Management strategy for ScotiaConnect, securing $12M in annual funding from executive stakeholders to modernize authentication infrastructure.

Full product specifications, Adobe Analytics dashboards, and CIAM strategy documentation available upon request under NDA.
