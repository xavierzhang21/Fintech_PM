ScotiaConnect — Payments & Digital Banking Products

Product owner of Scotiabank's business banking platform across EFT, Wire, Deposit, Statements, CIAM, and Adobe Analytics — serving 60,000 business customers.

Overview

As one of two Product Managers on ScotiaConnect — Scotiabank's digital banking platform for business customers — I led a 10-person agile team and owned the full product lifecycle across a broad portfolio of payment and banking products, serving 60,000 business customers.

EFT & Wire Payments

I managed the complete product lifecycle for EFT and Wire payment products — the core payment rails for Scotiabank business customers. I led cross-functional delivery and released the redesigned EFT and Wire payment flows to all 60,000 customers, increasing payments revenue by 6%.

Online Loan Statements

The Online Loan Statement had been the #1 NPS detractor for years — customers couldn't access their statements digitally and had to call the helpdesk. I engaged cross-functional teams and led my agile squad to ship the Online Loan Statement product, which:

  • Reduced ~500 manual hours of helpdesk work per month
  • Eliminated ~400 calls/month to the helpdesk
  • Generated ~$800,000 CAD per year in incremental revenue

GIC Online Purchase (Deposit Products)

I led the design and go-to-market strategy for the GIC online purchase feature — allowing small business customers to buy GICs digitally for the first time. The launch doubled the purchase flow conversion rate (100% increase).

The GIC feature is an example of where the hardest work was alignment — not engineering. Getting compliance, treasury, and digital to agree on the same flow took months of facilitated workshops. The conversion result validated every hour of that effort.

Adobe Analytics — First PM to Instrument ScotiaConnect

I was the first product owner to introduce Adobe Analytics to the ScotiaConnect application, initially tracking incoming wire flows for customers. I presented analytics insights directly to senior leadership and secured funding approval for a second iteration — which lifted the wire flow success rate from 5% to 80%.

CIAM Authentication — $12M Funding Secured

I conducted a full audit of the ScotiaConnect platform's authentication flows, mapped the UX gap between the existing experience and future technology innovation, and presented a comprehensive authentication strategy to leadership. The presentation secured $12 million in annual funding approval for the CIAM program.
