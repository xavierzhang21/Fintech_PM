CIBC Investor's Edge — Wealth Onboarding

End-to-end ownership of the onboarding experience for CIBC's flagship self-directed investing platform — driving conversion, securing a Visa partnership, and transforming delivery from Waterfall to Agile.

Organization CIBC · Commercial Banking & Wealth
Role Sr. Product Owner, Onboarding
Period August 2025 – Present
Impact +10% Conversion · 150K+ Customers/yr

Overview

CIBC Investor's Edge is one of Canada's leading self-directed investing platforms, serving retail investors who want to manage their own portfolios. As Sr. Product Owner, I own the full onboarding journey — both the internal-facing and external-facing flows — responsible for turning prospective investors into active, funded traders.

Product Management (50%)

I led the end-to-end product development lifecycle, introducing platform features that enabled comprehensive funnel analytics to measure onboarding effectiveness and surface user pain points through data-driven insights.

Key features shipped:

  • "Registration First" — decoupled account creation from the full onboarding form, dramatically reducing early drop-off by letting users establish a foothold before committing to the full flow
  • "Save and Resume" — allowed users to pause and return to incomplete applications, recovering previously lost conversions
  • "Freemium" — introduced a limited-access tier to reduce the barrier to entry and grow the funnel top

Together, these features drove a 10% uplift in user conversion and completion rates — validated through A/B testing and funnel analytics instrumented across the platform.

Cross-functional collaboration spanned Legal, AML, Compliance, Operations, and Technology — with process transparency improvements resulting in operational efficiency gains equivalent to 2 FTEs in cost savings.

Product Strategy (30%)

I designed and executed the vendor strategy for onboarding capabilities, which led to a landmark partnership with Visa. This integration enables new onboarding pathways projected to support 150,000+ customers annually with a projected ROI of 200% in the first year.

Business Transformation (20%)

I transformed the team's product delivery model from Waterfall to Agile within 2 months — improving speed-to-market by 30%. I launched a product dashboard and a monthly leadership showcase that significantly increased senior visibility into delivery progress and improved stakeholder alignment.

Detailed feature mockups, A/B test results, and funnel analytics available upon request under NDA.

Key Learnings

  • Progressive onboarding (registration-first, save/resume) converts more users than gating everything behind a single upfront form — the data confirmed what intuition suggested
  • Cross-functional trust is earned before the project starts, not during it — early and consistent stakeholder engagement unlocked faster compliance sign-off
  • Waterfall-to-Agile transformation is a people change before it's a process change — framing it around team autonomy and visibility accelerated adoption
