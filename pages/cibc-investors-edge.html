Digital Onboarding

Redesigning CIBC Investor's Edge onboarding end-to-end to drive conversion, reduce friction, and build a scalable foundation for wealth management growth.

Organization
CIBC Investor's Edge
Role
Sr. Product Owner, Onboarding
Period
Aug 2025 — Present
Impact
10% conversion uplift · 150K+ customers/yr

The Challenge

CIBC Investor's Edge serves over 150,000 new customers annually. Despite strong brand recognition, the onboarding funnel had significant drop-off, friction in account opening, and misalignment between the digital experience and evolving regulatory requirements.

10% uplift in user conversion · 200% projected ROI Year 1 from Visa partnership · 30% improvement in delivery speed after Waterfall→Agile in 2 months · 2 FTE cost savings from process transparency improvements

Approach

  • Led end-to-end product ownership of the onboarding funnel, coordinating across design, engineering, compliance, and operations.
  • Delivered high-impact features — "registration first," "save and resume," and "freemium" — through A/B testing and funnel analytics.
  • Initiated a Visa partnership unlocking a new acquisition channel projected at 150K+ customers/yr with 200% Year 1 ROI.
  • Drove Waterfall→Agile transformation for the squad, achieving 30% delivery speed improvement in 2 months.

Results

The redesigned onboarding reduced steps, improved form validation, and introduced progressive disclosure to reduce cognitive load. Process improvements eliminated 2 FTE of overhead, reallocated to higher-value discovery work.

Detailed product documentation, wireframes, and funnel analytics available upon request under NDA.
